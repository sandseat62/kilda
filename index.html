Healing is Possible

Post-concussion challenges in working memory are simply "roadblocks" on a map. Neuroplasticity means your brain is constantly reshaping itself based on your thoughts and actions.

By using your strengths in Art, Teaching, and Sports Logic, we are blazing new trails to bypass those roadblocks.

The 5-Step Neurocycle

Direct your own brain growth with intention.

1. Gather

Acknowledge memory signals without judgment.

2. Reflect

Ask "Why?" and explore the root of the load.

3. Write

Visualize the clutter and clear it out of your mind.

4. Recheck

Re-evaluate: How can we view this task differently?

5. Active Reach

Take specific actions to rewire the pathway.

Core Recovery Strategies

1. The Power Play Visualization

Use your sports intuition. Stop for 10 seconds. Visualize yourself "skating" through the task. Mentally "score" by seeing the completion.

2. The Artist's Palette

Verbal requests are abstract. Assign a color or texture to tasks. "Buy stamps" becomes a bright red square. This anchors data in your sensory cortex.

3. The Adirondack Loci System

Place items you need to remember along a favorite mental trail. Your spatial memory is robust; let the trailhead and creek "hold" your list.
